The World of Attack on Titan

The world of Attack on Titan is divided by gigantic walls and populated by both humans and mysterious Titans. Beyond the walls lies a dangerous unknown, while inside, society grapples with fear and oppression.

Paradis Island

Paradis Island is where the remnants of humanity reside, surrounded by the Walls—Maria, Rose, and Sina. It is the last stronghold against the Titans.

Marley

The Marleyan government controls a vast empire beyond the sea and holds Eldians in internment zones. They use Titans as weapons to maintain global dominance.

The Walls

The three massive Walls—Maria, Rose, and Sina—protect humanity from the Titans. These walls hide secrets that could change the fate of all who live within them.

The Underground City

A city built beneath Wall Sina, where the poor and criminals are forced to live. It is a dark, lawless place where even the Military Police rarely venture.

Shiganshina District

Shiganshina District is located in the south of Wall Maria. It is the site of the Colossal Titan’s first breach and serves as Eren’s hometown.

The Source of All Living Matter

Ymir Fritz gained the Power of the Titans from a glowing, tree-like anomaly.
