Main Characters

Eren Yeager

Eren Yeager is a determined young man who can transform into a Titan. He fights for humanity's survival but becomes a morally complex figure, willing to take extreme actions for freedom.

Mikasa Ackerman

Mikasa Ackerman is Eren Yeager's adoptive sister and a highly skilled soldier. Fiercely loyal and protective, she excels in combat and plays a crucial role in the fight for humanity's survival.

Armin Arlert

Armin Arlert, a close friend of Eren and Mikasa, is known for his intelligence and strategic thinking. Though physically weaker, his tactical brilliance makes him an invaluable asset in critical situations.

Jean Kirstein

Jean Kirstein is a pragmatic and loyal character who aspires to be a leader. He grows into a skilled fighter and strategist while balancing ambition with teamwork.

Levi Ackerman

Levi Ackerman is a stoic and highly skilled soldier known as humanity's strongest. He is deeply loyal to his team and plays a crucial role in the fight against Titans.

Sasha Blouse

Sasha Blouse is a cheerful soldier known for her love of food and marksmanship. Often called "Potato Girl," she combines lightheartedness with fierce loyalty and bravery in battle.

Connie Springer

Connie Springer is a friendly soldier known for his loyalty and sense of humor. He provides comic relief while showing bravery and determination in battle, deeply caring for his friends.

Reiner Braun

Reiner Braun is a strong, complex character torn between his roles as a soldier and Titan shifter. Initially loyal, he grapples with conflicting loyalties and the burdens of his choices.

Bertholdt Hoover

Bertholdt Hoover is a quiet, reserved individual who can transform into the Colossal Titan. He struggles with guilt over his role in devastating attacks, making him a tragic figure.

Annie Leonhart

Annie Leonhart is a skilled, stoic fighter who can transform into the Female Titan. She hides her identity while struggling with her mission and personal feelings.

Ymir Fritz

Ymir is an independent character with the power to become the Jaw Titan. She seeks freedom and shows loyalty to Historia.

Historia Reiss

Historia Reiss is the compassionate heir to the royal family. Initially timid, she becomes a strong leader while remaining loyal to Ymir.

Hannes
